About Shark Club!

Discover more about sharks

With fossil records dating back 400 million years, sharks have outlived the dinosaurs and many other forms of life currently on earth.

There are more than 1,000 species of sharks and rays, with new species discovered every year.

They are very essential to the natural order of marine ecosystems but now they are facing extinction!

Here is how YOU can help in preserving them:

  • Learn more about them
  • Join organizations that help them
  • Consider donating
